Facilities of Pegasus Studios

The Pegasus apartment studio rooms are all self-contained for all your needs. As well as the obligatory bed and wardrobe, each room has cooking facilities, a fridge and a bar. The bathrooms are all equipped with a bath and a shower and each room has its own private balcony.

If you don't fancy cooking breakfast in a morning, the pegasus bar downstairs offers an excellent cooked english breakfast or continental along with omelettes, toasties and baguettes. If you fancy a chilled relaxed night time drink, the bar has two big screen tv's to show sports action as it happens and there's a quiz and a game of bingo every night to liven things up!
